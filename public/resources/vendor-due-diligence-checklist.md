# Vendor Due Diligence Checklist (HIPAA-related)

Use this checklist to assess third-party vendors that may access or process PHI.

## Contract & Legal
- Business Associate Agreement (BAA) executed and current
- Data Processing Agreement (DPA) if applicable
- Sub-processor list available and contractual flow-down of obligations
- Breach notification timelines and SLAs

## Security & Compliance
- SOC 2 Type II or ISO 27001 certification (evidence provided)
- HIPAA program details and responsibility matrix (shared responsibility)
- Security policies (acceptable use, access control, incident response)
- Employee background checks and security training

## Technical Controls
- MFA for all privileged access; SSO support
- Encryption at rest and in transit (TLS 1.2+)
- Network segmentation and least-privilege access
- Vulnerability management and regular penetration testing
- Centralized logging and monitoring with alerting

## Privacy & Data Handling
- Data residency/location disclosure
- Data minimization and retention policy
- Secure data deletion and export options
- Data subject requests and audit support

## Operations & Resilience
- Backup strategy and restore testing
- Disaster recovery objectives (RPO/RTO)
- Change management and deployment controls
- Support model and escalation paths
